About Me

My name is Cherian Mathew, born on 6th April  1979.I have completed my graduation in B.Com. I'm an Independent Computer Software Professional in Coimbatore. I was born and brought up in a Christian family in a Brethern background.

About my education:

I did my schooling in Pandiraj  Memorial School in Kotagiri, Immaculate heart of Mary's Matriculation Hr Sec School in Veerandy and GKD Martic Hr Sec School in Perianaikenpalayam. After my schooling I joined Annamalai University where I completed my B.Com. 

My work experience:

Worked as VB Programmer in Visual Ads, Coimbatore for six months from August 2001 to January 2002. After that I joined Polymath software technologies, Coimbatore and worked as a Visual Foxpro application developer for 2 years and 8 months till October 2004. I joined Allsec technologies in December 2004 and worked as a Junior Programmer in the IT department till August 2007. After that I became an Independent Computer Software Professional in Coimbatore. Presently I'm working in .NET and SQL server.

My family:

I have a brother elder to me and three sisters. My brother is a Sound Engineer working in CBN india, Hyderabad. My elder sister is running an orphanage for children. I have two younger sisters out of which one is married to an Evangelist in Mumbai and the other working in Mumbai as a Design Engineer.
