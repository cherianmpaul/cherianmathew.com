How do I get your email address ?

If you want my email address, just go to the Contact Me page and select the first option. There you will have to enter your email address and I will send an email to your email address.

Where can I give my comments about this website ?

If you want to give me a general comment about the site you can use the Guest Book, where you can give your comments and other people can view your comments. If you want to send a personal feed back you can use the feed back form and send feed back directly to me.

Back to Top

How can I send instant message to you from your website?

If you want to send personal instant messages to me, please use the instant message form in the contact me page. There you can send me a message which will be delivered to my email address. I will reply to it as soon as possible.

Back to Top

I don't have an email address. How can I contact you ?

If you don't have an email address you can use the instant message form to send messages to me or you can request my residential address from the address request form in the contact me page.

Back to Top

Do I require a chat ID to chat in your web site ?

No. To chat in my website you can just log in to the chat session with your name from the Chat page and start chatting. You will require JAVA installed in your computer to enable the chat.

Back to Top

Is there any way that I can ask my friends to visit your site ?

Yes. You can use the Tell a Friend form to inform your friend about my website. When you submit that page, with your friends email address, he will receive an email asking him to visit my website.
